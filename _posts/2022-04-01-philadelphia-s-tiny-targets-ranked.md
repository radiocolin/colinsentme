## Washington Square (🪦)

The worst one. Incredibly tiny, and for some reason floor space is given up to have a CVS and a Starbucks inside, when there’s standalone ones within a block.  

## Broad and Washington

Decent size, but has trouble keeping things in stock. 

## Rittenhouse

Good location, and a good focus on essentials. Large food selection, good pharmacy department, and a surprisingly decent home goods section. 

## Art Museum

Great size, good selection, but you may die trying to get through the intersection of 20th and Callowhill. 

## Northern Liberties

Basically a full-size Target. Wide selection. The best one.
